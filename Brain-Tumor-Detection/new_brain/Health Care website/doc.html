Neuroradiologist

A neuroradiologist is a doctor who specializes in the use of medical imaging to diagnose and treat conditions of the nervous system, including the brain. Neuroradiologists may interpret MRI or CT scans to detect the presence of brain tumors, and may perform specialized imaging studies, such as magnetic resonance spectroscopy, to help determine the type of tumor
